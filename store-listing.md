## App Name
mesoHungry

## Short Description (80 chars max)
Swipe to discover restaurants nearby. Like, compare, and pick where to eat.

## Full Description (4000 chars max)
Can't decide where to eat? mesoHungry makes it easy.

Swipe through nearby restaurants like a dating app — swipe right to save places you like, swipe left to skip. When you've saved enough, mesoHungry helps you pick the perfect spot.

HOW IT WORKS
- Open the app and let it find restaurants near you
- Swipe through restaurant cards with photos, ratings, price levels, and cuisine tags
- Swipe right to like, left to skip
- Browse your liked list and tap any restaurant for full details — hours, phone, website, and more
- After every 10 likes, a decision helper screen appears to help you narrow down your choice

FEATURES
- Tinder-style swiping for restaurants
- Adjustable search radius (0.5 to 5 miles)
- Filter by cuisine type (multi-select)
- Filter out fast food and convenience stores
- Set your location via GPS, search, or map pin
- View full restaurant details: photos, hours, phone, website, ratings
- Decision helper to pick from your liked restaurants
- Beautiful, nature-inspired design
- No account required — fully private

PRIVACY FIRST
mesoHungry does not require an account. Your liked restaurants are stored only on your device. We use anonymous analytics to improve the app and never sell your data.

Powered by Google Places.
